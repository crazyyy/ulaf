About ULAF

The Ukrainian League of American Football (ULAF) was established to promote and develop American Football in Ukraine, providing a competitive platform for athletes and fostering the growth of the sport nationwide.

Our league brings together passionate players, dedicated coaches, and enthusiastic fans to create an authentic American Football experience. We are committed to maintaining high standards of competition while promoting sportsmanship, teamwork, and athletic excellence.
