About ULAF

The Ukrainian League of American Football (ULAF) is the governing body for American football in Ukraine, overseeing competitions, development programs, and the growth of the sport nationwide.

Founded in 2011, ULAF has grown to include 12 teams across Ukraine, with hundreds of athletes competing at various levels from youth to senior competitions.
