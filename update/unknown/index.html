About ULAF

Building American Football Excellence in Ukraine

The Ukrainian League of American Football (ULAF) is the premier organization dedicated to developing and promoting American football throughout Ukraine. Since our establishment, we have been committed to fostering athletic excellence, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

Our mission is to provide a competitive platform for Ukrainian athletes to showcase their talents while building character, discipline, and leadership skills. We believe in the power of sport to unite communities and inspire the next generation of champions.
