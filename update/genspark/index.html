About ULAF

The Ukrainian League of American Football (ULAF) is the premier American football league in Ukraine, bringing together the best teams and players from across the country.

Founded with a vision to promote American football in Ukraine, ULAF has grown into a competitive league that showcases exceptional talent, sportsmanship, and athletic excellence. Our mission is to develop the sport at all levels while fostering a strong community of players, coaches, and fans.
