About ULAF Ukrainian League of American Football

The Ukrainian League of American Football (ULAF) is the premier organization dedicated to developing and promoting American football throughout Ukraine. Since our establishment, we have been committed to building a strong foundation for the sport while fostering athletic excellence and community engagement.

Our league brings together talented athletes, dedicated coaches, and passionate fans to create an authentic American football experience. We provide a platform for players to showcase their skills, compete at the highest level, and represent their teams with pride and determination.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To develop, promote, and organize American football in Ukraine by providing competitive opportunities, fostering athletic excellence, and building a strong community of players, coaches, and fans who share a passion for the sport.

Our Vision

To establish Ukraine as a recognized force in European American football, creating pathways for Ukrainian athletes to compete internationally while inspiring the next generation of football players and fans across the nation.
